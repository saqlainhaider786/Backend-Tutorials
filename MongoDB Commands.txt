==> mongosh - it is used to launch mongo DB.

==> show dbs - used to see the list of db.

==> use <db_name> - used to switch to another db.

==> show collections - shows the existing documents in the db.

==> db.coll_name.find() - Used to find any data from the db.

==> db.coll_name.insert() - used to insert data within any collection.
